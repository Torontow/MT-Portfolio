About Michael

Yes, Torontow is my real name.

Stage names abound in this industry, but mine is not one. If I knew what my name originally was before my paternal great-great grandparents fled the Russian Empire in 1905, I’d let you know. But for now, I am Michael Torontow, a proud Canadian actor who has enjoyed a career that spans more than two decades. And while my name causes daily headaches, I love it and wouldn’t change it for the world.
